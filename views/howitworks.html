How Kira Works

Kira is a social enterprise focused on
delivering Kirawawa, a survival kit for low
income mothers of new-borns in Peru

Our process

1. Identification

Kira engages with local hospitals to identify low income mothers

2. Selection

We interview and select mothers between 3 and 6 months of pregnancy

3. Fundraising

We start our fundraising process through our website and private rounds

4. Baby’s health

The mother attends prenatal checkups at the hospital – one per month until birth

5. Kirawawa delivery

We close the funding and deliver Kirawawa when the baby is born

6. Update

Our donors get un update of the baby and mother after birth
